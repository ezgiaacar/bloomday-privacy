01 — Overview

Simple by design

BloomDay is a task tracker and virtual garden app. We collect only what is necessary to run the app. We do not sell data, build profiles, or share your information with advertisers.

We believe privacy should be the default, not a setting you have to find. This policy is written to be read, not to obscure. If anything is unclear, write to us.

02 — Data We Collect

What we know about you

BloomDay collects a minimal amount of information to operate.

Account information

When you create an account, we collect your email address and a securely hashed password. This is used solely to authenticate your identity and provide access to the app. We do not collect your name, phone number, or any other personal identifier unless you choose to set a display name inside the app.

App data

Your tasks, garden progress, plant inventory, streak history, and in-app preferences are stored to provide a seamless experience across sessions and devices. This data is tied to your account and not shared with anyone.

Weather data

The app fetches your local weather once per day to apply real-world conditions to your garden. This request uses your device's approximate location. No location data is stored on our servers — it is used in the moment and discarded.

◉

We do not collect device identifiers, IP addresses, usage statistics, crash telemetry, or any other form of analytics. There is no tracking of how you use the app beyond what is necessary to sync your data.

03 — How We Use It

Only what runs the app

We use your data for one purpose: to make BloomDay work for you.

  • 🔐 Authentication. Your email and credentials are used to create and protect your account. We use Supabase for secure authentication.
  • 🌿 Sync. Your tasks, plants, and progress are saved so your garden persists across sessions and devices.
  • 🔔 Notifications. If you enable task reminders, the app schedules local notifications on your device. These are not sent through a server — they fire locally and contain only the task text you wrote.
  • ✉️ Account communications. We may use your email address to notify you of critical account events (password reset, deletion confirmation). We do not send marketing emails.

04 — Advertising

Ads on the free plan

The free plan includes occasional ads served by Google AdMob. Ads appear at most once per day on app launch. They are never shown during Focus Mode.

Non-personalised ads

BloomDay requests non-personalised ads only. Google AdMob does not build a behavioural profile on you through this app. Ads are contextual, not targeted to your personal data.

Remove ads entirely

Upgrading to any paid plan — Bloom, Forest, or Eden — removes all ads permanently for the duration of your subscription. No ads are ever shown to paid subscribers.

📄

Google AdMob operates under its own privacy policy. You can review it at policies.google.com/privacy. BloomDay does not pass any personally identifiable information to AdMob.

05 — Third Parties

Who else touches your data

We use a small number of trusted infrastructure providers to operate the app. Your data is never sold, rented, or shared for marketing purposes.

Supabase

We use Supabase for account authentication and database storage. Your email address and app data are stored on Supabase's infrastructure. Supabase complies with GDPR and is SOC 2 Type II certified. Data is stored in the EU (Frankfurt).

RevenueCat

Subscriptions (Bloom, Forest, Eden) are managed through RevenueCat and processed by Apple's App Store. RevenueCat receives a transaction token to verify your subscription status. No payment card data passes through our servers.

Google AdMob

Free-plan users may see ads served by Google AdMob. BloomDay requests non-personalised ads only. See the Advertising section above for full details.

No other third parties have access to your data. We do not use Facebook, Amplitude, Mixpanel, Firebase Analytics, Sentry, or any other analytics or crash-reporting service.

06 — Storage & Retention

Where your data lives

Your account information and app data are retained for as long as your account is active.

  • 💾 Active accounts. All data is retained to keep your garden intact between sessions. Nothing is automatically deleted while your account exists.
  • 📱 Deleting the app. Removing the app from your device does not delete your account or data from our servers. Your garden will be waiting if you reinstall.
  • 🗑️ Account deletion. You can delete your account directly from within the app (Settings → Delete Account). This permanently removes your email, tasks, plants, and all associated data. Deletion is irreversible.
⏱

Following a deletion request, data is removed from our active database within 72 hours and purged from backups within 30 days.

07 — Your Rights

What you can ask of us

Under GDPR and applicable privacy laws, you have the following rights regarding your personal data.

Access

Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you. We will provide it in a readable format within 30 days.

Correction

Request correction of any inaccurate information associated with your account.

Deletion

Request permanent deletion of your account and all associated data. This can be done directly in the app or by contacting us.

Portability

Export your task data at any time from Settings → Export My Data. The export is a CSV file you can keep or import elsewhere.

08 — Children's Privacy

Not for children under 13

BloomDay is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has created an account, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

09 — Contact

Questions or requests

If you have any questions about this policy, want to request access to your data, or need to report a concern, please write to us. We respond to all privacy enquiries within 5 business days.
