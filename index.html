Overview

BloomDay ("the App") is a task tracker and virtual garden app. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your rights.

Data We Collect

BloomDay collects the following information:

  • Account information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and sign-in credentials. This information is required to authenticate your account and provide access to the App.
  • App data: Your tasks, garden progress, plant inventory, and preferences are stored to provide you with a seamless experience across sessions.

How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To authenticate your sign-in and keep your account secure.
  • To save and sync your tasks, garden progress, and preferences.
  • To communicate with you about your account or important updates to the App (if necessary).

No Analytics or Tracking

The App does not use any analytics services, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies. We do not collect usage statistics, device identifiers, IP addresses, or any other form of telemetry.

No Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or disclose your personal information to third parties. Your data is only used to operate the App as described in this policy.

Data Storage and Retention

Your account information and app data are retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete the App from your device, your account and associated data may still be stored on our servers. To permanently delete your data, please contact us using the details below and we will process your request.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of any inaccurate information.
  • Request permanent deletion of your account and all associated data.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email address below.

Children's Privacy

BloomDay is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the revised version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
